About Us

At Kevaat, we help 12th-grade students make the right college decision by connecting them directly with students from private universities in India. Instead of relying on scattered online information, students can book a 30-minute one-on-one video call with a real university student to get honest, firsthand insights about life at that college.

What We Offer

If a 12th grader is choosing between private universities, we make their decision easier. Talk directly to students from those universities and ask about:

  • Campus Life: Clubs, activities, hostels, and student life.
  • Academics: Quality education, instructors, coursework, and internships.
  • Career Opportunities: Job opportunities, industry exposure, and alumni network.
  • Student Experience: Learn about the positive and negative aspects of studying there from current students.

How It Works

  1. Choose the private institutions you're considering.
  2. We match you with student mentors.
  3. Secure payment via Razorpay.
  4. Gain valuable insights in a 30-minute Zoom meeting.
  5. Make an Informed Choice - Use real-world experiences to make confident decisions.

Payment & Security

Kevaat provides a smooth and safe payment experience:

  • Secure Transactions - Razorpay processes payments using encryption to ensure safety.
  • Transparent Pricing: You simply pay for your mentorship session, with no additional fees.
  • Mentor Payout Assurance: Mentors receive compensated after successfully finishing the session.
  • Refund and cancellation policy available.
