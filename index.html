Frequently Asked Questions

What is Bridge Peer Consulting? ▼

Bridge Peer Consulting helps you talk to students who are already studying in the colleges you’re interested in. Through a 30-minute video call, you can ask questions and get real, honest answers about college life, academics, and everything you want to know.

Why should I trust the information shared by the mentors? ▼

Our mentors are real students currently studying in those colleges. They share their personal experiences, not paid reviews. So, you get genuine, up-to-date information that can help you make the right decision.

Can I speak to mentors from multiple colleges? ▼

Yes! If you're confused between a few colleges, you can talk to mentors from each one. You can speak to as many mentors as you want, with each additional session giving you a 30-minute video call with a mentor from a different college to help you make a better decision.

How do I become a mentor?? ▼

If you’re already studying in a college and want to help others while earning some money, you can join as a mentor. Just go to our 'Join as Mentor' section, fill out the form, and we’ll get back to you.

What if I’m not satisfied with the session? ▼

We want you to have a good experience. If you didn’t find the session helpful, just let us know within 24 hours, and we’ll try our best to fix the issue
