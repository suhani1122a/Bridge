Frequently Asked Questions

What is BRIDGE? ▼

BRIDGE is a mentorship platform that connects aspiring students with current international university students for guidance and insights on studying abroad.

What topics can I discuss during the session? ▼

You can ask about university life, admission processes, course details, accommodation, scholarships, cultural adjustments, and any other concerns related to studying abroad.

How are the mentors selected? ▼

Our mentors are verified students currently enrolled in foreign universities. They have been vetted to ensure they provide accurate and helpful insights.

How does the consultation process work? ▼

Students can book a 30-minute call with a mentor assigned by us based on the destination they wish to visit. After payment, we will schedule the session and provide all the details for your virtual meeting.

What is the cost of a consultation session? ▼

Each 30-minute session costs ₹599 (or equivalent in local currency). Payment is required upfront to confirm the booking.

What makes our mentors unique compared to traditional counselors? ▼

Unlike traditional counselors, our mentors are recent students who have successfully navigated the same process they’re now guiding you through. This makes them relatable, adaptable to your learning style, and deeply committed to your success. They’re not just trained—they’ve lived the experience.
