Privacy Policy

At Kevaat, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to facilitate mentorship sessions:

a. Personal Information (Provided by You)

  • Name, email, phone number (for registration and communication)
  • University name (to match you with the right mentor)
  • How we can help (to understand your requirements)
  • Preferred date & time for a session (collected via Calendly)

b. Information Collected via Third-Party Services

  • Calendly: Used for scheduling, which collects your name and email ID to book a session.
  • Razorpay: Used for payment processing. We do not store any payment details; all transactions are securely handled by Razorpay.

c. Automatically Collected Data

  • Device information (IP address, browser type, operating system)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent on the platform)

Note: We do not use cookies, tracking technologies, or analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Register you for mentorship sessions
